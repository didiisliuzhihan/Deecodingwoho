🧩INTRODUCTION🧩

A game that captures with facial recognition!

🕹️How to play: You just need to move! Especially your nose!

🎯Game goal: Collect Halloween candy! Oh wait, and twist your neck as much as possible to avoid bombs!

🥉There are three levels in total:

Level 1: Collect 10 candies!

Level 2: Twist your neck to avoid the bombs! Protect your candy!

Level 3: Ops! Candies and bombs are coming together! Are you a Halloween winner or will you go home crying?

🐽Other setting: Swipe to the bottom of the screen to switch to the piggy nose mode!
